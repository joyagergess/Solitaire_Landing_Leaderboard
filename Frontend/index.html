Game Rules

Objective: Stack all cards in each suit from Ace to King in the foundation piles.
Gameplay: Move cards between columns in descending order and alternating colors to uncover all hidden cards.
Winning: Complete every foundation pile by stacking all cards in order from Ace to King.
